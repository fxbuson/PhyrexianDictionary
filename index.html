This is Osmago's prototype/sandbox website for the phyrexian dictionary v3. Built from the files in my fork of the main repo.

For now we have the bulk of the words on MTGWiki translated to this format. Checkout entries and samples below

If you have any issues or suggestions, feel free to reach me on reddit or discord, and raise issues on the GitHub repo. Also feel free to fork this repo and do whatever you want, as long as it's all free and open.
